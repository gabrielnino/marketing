- News that is purely informational is discarded.

- “Where can I get that jersey?”

- “Do you know where to find it?”

Rules:
- If a news item requires a direct sales CTA → discard.
- If the purchase curiosity emerges naturally from identity or emotion → prioritize.

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------

- WhatsApp GROUPS
- Tone: “Hincha informado” (credible, conversational, non-marketing)

Constraints:
- 1–3 short lines
- Ends with a clear open question
- Sounds like a real fan, not a media outlet or brand

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 6 — FINAL CURATION
--------------------------------------------------
From all evaluated candidates, CURATE the TOP 5 items that:
- Best satisfy emotional priorities (A/B/C)
- Are current or contextually relevant
- Encourage group conversation
- Prepare the ground for implicit purchase pull
